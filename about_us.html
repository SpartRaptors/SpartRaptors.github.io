ABOUT US
Welcome to GoLocal, your number one source for all things traditional. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of traditional Indian foods, with a focus on promoting the Indian food market to Global buyers.

GoLocal has a long way to go, as it is still in the development stages. Our passion for helping other people in our community drove us to set up this app as a B2C (Business to Consumer) platform. We are now thrilled to become a part of the eCommerce food industry, but as of now this app is not used for any sort of commercial / monetization purpose.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
